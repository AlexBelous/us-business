Our Services

End-to-end laboratory equipment solutions — from sourcing to decommissioning

Equipment Sales

New and pre-owned analytical instruments from leading OEMs. HPLC, GC, LC/MS, GC/MS, spectrophotometers, autosamplers, and complete systems.

Repair & Maintenance

On-site and bench/depot repair for chromatography, mass spectrometry, and spectroscopy. Fast turnaround, multi-vendor coverage.

Preventive Maintenance

Scheduled PM contracts to extend instrument life and minimize downtime. Flexible plans from single visits to annual programs.

Calibration & Commissioning

Installation, IQ/OQ/PQ qualification, calibration, and operator training. Compliance-ready documentation for regulated environments.

Refurbished Instruments

Professionally reconditioned equipment tested to OEM specifications. Cost-effective alternative to new with reliable, verified performance.

Spare Parts & Consumables

OEM and compatible parts, columns, fittings, lamps, seals, and consumables. Fast global sourcing with competitive pricing.

Consignment & Trade-In

Sell or trade surplus lab instruments. We handle valuation, marketing, and buyer coordination to maximize your return on idle assets.

Remote Technical Support

On-demand remote diagnostics, troubleshooting, and software support. Quick resolution for system errors and performance issues.

Lab Relocation

Professional instrument deinstallation, packing, transport, and reinstallation. We handle logistics so you can focus on science.

Bench & Depot Repair

Ship your instrument to us for cost-effective bench repair at our facility. We provide shipping materials and packing instructions for safe transport. Ideal for labs outside our immediate area or for complex repairs requiring extended diagnostics.

On-site repairs also available — our engineers come to your lab with parts and tools to get you back online fast.
